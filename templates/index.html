Movie Data Collection

For this project, we will be collecting anonymous data on location and activities that can be tracked through your phone. For example, the moves app will record steps, where you are walking, as well as things like transit / driving. We will assign each person's data a unique randomized ID and release a dataset of all data to the class.

First, you need to download the app (it is available on iOS and Android). After downloading the app and setting it up for the first time, come back to this site to grant us authorization

Once you have the downloaded the app, please click on the following link to grant us authorization to collect your data. You don't create an account with Moves, so we won't know who you are and the only thing we will store will be the access token (not date of permission, etc). After clicking on the button, you will be prompted to open the app and then it will ask for your permissions. After you accept, you will be redirected to a webpage on this site that should have an indication of success - if this is not the case, please contact Professor Field and we will get it resolved quickly.
